# Amazon Cognito

* "wrapper" around many different authentication technologies.
  (similar in concept to KeyCloak)

* "developer-centric customer identity and access management (CIAM) service.
  It can be used to store final identities or federate authentication to
  third party services. (eg. social identity providers, SAML, OpenID Connect
  - OIDC- providers, ...)

* Out of the box, it integrates with an extended ecosystem of front-end and
  back-end development resources and SDK libraries.

* When used as priamry identities storage, flows exists to create users with
  login of type "name" or "email" and the email scenario, whether users can
  autoregister (by providing their own email) or they can not (only an admin
  can add new email identities). Also support for e-mail confirmation, ...
  is provided out of the box.

* It also integrates with other AWS Services that will use Cognito as Identity
  provider. This is the case for example with ELB, allows to add authentication
  based on Cognito.
