##  AWS CDK (Infra as Code)

- deprecates CloudFormation [[IaC.CloudFormation,PM.RISK.deprecation]].<br/>
  **10 lines AWS-CDK produces a 500 lines CloudFormation config file**.
  <https://docs.aws.amazon.com/cdk/latest/guide/home.html> [[PM.low_code,doc_has.comparative]]

- Cloud Development Kit (CDK) for Terraform:
  By AWS, HashiCorp, and Terrastack
  - <https://www.infoq.com/news/2020/07/cdk-terraform/>

  ```
  |Python    |
  |Typescript| -> (input) -> CDKTF -> |terraform json| -> terraform apply
  |...       | 
  ```
- CDKTF also supports to deploy resources to AWS, Azure, Google Cloud. [[cloud.Azure,cloud.GCP]]
