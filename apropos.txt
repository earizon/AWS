## Q: Why do I need this if we already have Claude?

A: simply put, some/many coding recipes here have become outdated
with the "powerful" LLMs BUT ...
- sometime LLMs guide us the wrong way because we wrote the wrong
  PROMPT.
- LLMs guides us through the most standard way to do things, which
  many times is not the best way to work.
- LLMs cost money :D. This site does not.
- You can easily extend this page with private content that 
  public LLMs are just not aware of. No need for complex fine-tunning
  or vector databases, ... just markdown (plain text) and tags.

- content returned from LLMs ussually differ from the content
  returning by this page due to the "unknown unkowns". You can for
  example query about "JAVA QA" topics to the LLM and the filter
  content in this page related to QA. The LLMs offers very
  targeted and meaningful information, ... but still sometimes it
  ignores other important information. Ex:
  -  You can spend years asking Claude to do stuff while ignoring
     that you could have done that same much faster using Cloud Custodian,
