[[{troubleshooting.DNS]]
* Simptom: dig mydomain.com returns no results, but the domain has been
  properly registered.
* Probably cause: Amazon suspended the domain. It is no active anymore.

  """ ...Why was the domain suspended? ...  Beginning January 1st, 2014, 
  ICANN requires that registrars verify, within 15 days after 
  registration, that the registrant contact for a domain can be 
  reached. We didn't get a response to our initial email for 
  verification that the email address is valid. ..."

* FIX:  Go to Route 53 ··> "Registered domains" and check Contact Information.
  * Registrant Contact must be in state "Verified".
